# 03 — Spaceports and Launchers
## AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10 · IDEALE-ESG · A-Aerospace · I-Infrastructures

## 1. Section Purpose

This section covers the **ground infrastructure** for launch, re-entry, and recovery operations of the **AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10 (Q10)**. It addresses spaceport facilities, launcher interfaces, propellant and energy systems, crew and payload processing, mission control interfaces, safety / security / rescue infrastructure, and post-flight turnaround and reuse. Traceability and governance close the section.

# 05-08 — Predictive Maintenance Explainability and Evidence
## AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10

---

## 1. Scope

This sub-section covers **Explainable AI (XAI) methods, evidence generation frameworks, and human-machine interface (HMI) design** for the predictive maintenance NN system of the AMPEL360-PLUS Q10.

Explainability is a prerequisite for certification acceptance, crew and operator trust, and auditability of NN-generated maintenance decisions.

---

## 2. Objectives

- Define the XAI requirements for each NN module within the predictive maintenance system.
- Select and implement appropriate XAI techniques (SHAP, LIME, attention maps, saliency analysis, physics-informed constraints).
- Design the HMI for maintenance crew and ground operators to interpret and act on NN outputs.
- Produce evidence artefacts (explanation logs, confidence metrics, uncertainty quantification) for the safety case and certification dossier.

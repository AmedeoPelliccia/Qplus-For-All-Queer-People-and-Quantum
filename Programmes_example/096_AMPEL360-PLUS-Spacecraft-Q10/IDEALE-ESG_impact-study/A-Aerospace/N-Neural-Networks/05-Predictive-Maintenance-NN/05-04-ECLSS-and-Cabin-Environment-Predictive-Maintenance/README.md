# 05-04 — ECLSS and Cabin Environment Predictive Maintenance
## AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10

---

## 1. Scope

This sub-section covers **NN-based predictive maintenance for the Environmental Control and Life Support System (ECLSS) and cabin environment** of the AMPEL360-PLUS Q10.

It addresses the monitoring and prognosis of cabin atmosphere management, water recovery, CO2 removal, thermal comfort, and crew safety systems.

---

## 2. Objectives

- Apply NN-based anomaly detection and prognosis to ECLSS subsystems.
- Predict degradation of CO2 scrubbing, O2 generation, water recovery, and filtration systems.
- Monitor cabin pressure, temperature, humidity, and air quality with NN-assisted trend prediction.
- Generate maintenance schedules for consumable replenishment and component replacement before crew risk thresholds are reached.

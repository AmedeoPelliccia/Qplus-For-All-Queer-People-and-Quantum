# 05-07 — Turnaround, Reuse and Maintenance Scheduling NN
## AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10

---

## 1. Scope

This sub-section covers **NN-based optimisation of turnaround operations, reuse eligibility assessment, and maintenance scheduling** for the AMPEL360-PLUS Q10 between flights.

The goal is to minimise turnaround time while ensuring vehicle airworthiness and safety, supporting the spaceplane's high-reuse mission model.

---

## 2. Objectives

- Apply NN scheduling optimisation to sequence post-landing inspection, refurbishment, and relaunch preparation tasks.
- Use aggregated health and RUL data (from 05-02 through 05-06) to generate reuse eligibility decisions.
- Optimise maintenance resource allocation (workforce, tools, parts) to minimise turnaround duration.
- Produce a machine-generated maintenance work-order package traceable to flight health data.

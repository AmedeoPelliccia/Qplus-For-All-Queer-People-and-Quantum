# 05-06 — Reentry Loads, Fatigue and Remaining Useful Life
## AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10

---

## 1. Scope

This sub-section covers **NN-based estimation of reentry loads accumulation, fatigue consumption, and Remaining Useful Life (RUL)** for structures and key components of the AMPEL360-PLUS Q10.

RUL estimation is the primary output that informs retirement, refurbishment, or continued service decisions for the reusable spaceplane.

---

## 2. Objectives

- Model cumulative reentry aerodynamic and thermal load history per flight.
- Apply NN-based fatigue models to predict damage accumulation across structure, TPS, and propulsion components.
- Estimate RUL for each component and assembly, with confidence intervals, to support go/no-go reuse decisions.
- Provide lifecycle gate evidence compatible with the programme's safety case and certification baseline.

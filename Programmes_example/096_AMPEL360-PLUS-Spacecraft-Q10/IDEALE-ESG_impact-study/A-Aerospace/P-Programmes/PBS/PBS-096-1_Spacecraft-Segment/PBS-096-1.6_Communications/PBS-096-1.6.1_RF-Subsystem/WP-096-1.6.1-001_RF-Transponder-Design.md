# WP-096-1.6.1-001 — RF Transponder Design

**Section:** PBS-096-1.6.1  
**Programme:** AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10 (PBS-096)

## Purpose

S-band and X-band transponder design for TT&C, including link budget, EIRP, and frequency coordination with ITU and national authorities.

## Scope

This work package defines the requirements, design approach, verification strategy, and evidence artefacts for rf transponder design.

# WP-096-1.11.1-001 — Quantum Sensing Payload Integration

**Section:** PBS-096-1.11.1  
**Programme:** AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10 (PBS-096)

## Purpose

Integration concept and interface definition for quantum sensing payloads (atom interferometers, NV-centre magnetometers, SQUID arrays) aboard the crewed spacecraft.

## Scope

This work package defines the requirements, design approach, verification strategy, and evidence artefacts for quantum sensing payload integration.

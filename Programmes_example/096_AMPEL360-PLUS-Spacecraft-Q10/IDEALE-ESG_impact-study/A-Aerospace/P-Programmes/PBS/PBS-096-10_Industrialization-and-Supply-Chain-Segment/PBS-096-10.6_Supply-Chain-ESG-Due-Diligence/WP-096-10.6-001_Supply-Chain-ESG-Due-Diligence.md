# WP-096-10.6-001 — Supply Chain ESG Due Diligence

**Section:** PBS-096-10.6  
**Programme:** AMPEL360-PLUS Spacecraft Q10 (PBS-096)

## Purpose

Supply chain ESG due diligence programme covering environmental, social, and governance criteria for all tier-1 and critical tier-2 suppliers aligned with EU CSDDD and IDEALE-S/G axes.

## Scope

This work package defines the requirements, design approach, verification strategy, and evidence artefacts for supply chain esg due diligence.

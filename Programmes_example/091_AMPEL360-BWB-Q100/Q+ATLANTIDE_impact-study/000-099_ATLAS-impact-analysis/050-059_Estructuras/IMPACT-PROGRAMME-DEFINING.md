## Programme-Defining Statement

The 050-059 group is **programme-defining** for the AMPEL360 BWB Q100 (BWB-Q100) aircraft.
This group drives the programme identity, certification baseline, and key performance metrics.

Key programme-defining characteristics:
- BWB configuration creates unique structural and systems integration challenges
- LH₂/PEMFC/battery hybrid-electric propulsion architecture
- Novel materials and manufacturing processes for BWB primary structure
- S1000D CSDB data modules in this group require dedicated BREX/SNS/DMRL definitions

# Impact Assessment: ▲ Heavy — 040-049 Aviónica, Información y APU

**Programme:** AMPEL360 BWB Q100 (BWB-Q100)  
**Aircraft concept:** BWB H₂ Hy-E Q100 INTEGRA  
**Group:** 040-049 — Aviónica, Información y APU  
**Impact level:** ▲ Heavy — Significant programme-specific changes driven by BWB geometry, HVDC architecture, or LH₂ propulsion integration.

## Impact Statement

The 040-049 group carries heavy programme-impact for the AMPEL360 BWB Q100 (BWB-Q100) aircraft due to:
- Blended Wing Body (BWB) non-conventional internal layout
- HVDC hybrid-electric power architecture (270 V / 540 V busses)
- LH₂ cryogenic fuel system and ATEX zone definitions
- Novel BWB flight-control and structural load-path interactions

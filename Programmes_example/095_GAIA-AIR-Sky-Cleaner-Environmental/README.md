# Programme Example — GAIA-AIR Sky Cleaner Environmental (GAIA-AIR-SCE)

## Overview

The **GAIA-AIR Sky Cleaner Environmental (GAIA-AIR-SCE)** is a dedicated environmental and atmospheric remediation aircraft programme. It is designed to conduct airborne clean-up missions including contrail mitigation, direct air capture, atmospheric monitoring, and cloud-seeding or pollution-abatement operations.

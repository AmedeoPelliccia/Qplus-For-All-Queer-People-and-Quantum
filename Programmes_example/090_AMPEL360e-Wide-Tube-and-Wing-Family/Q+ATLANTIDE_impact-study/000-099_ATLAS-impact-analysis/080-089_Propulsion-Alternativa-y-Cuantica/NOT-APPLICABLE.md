# NOT APPLICABLE: 080-089 — Propulsión Alternativa y Cuántica

**Programme:** AMPEL360e Wide Tube-and-Wing Family (eWTW)

## Applicability Statement

The ATA chapter range **080–089** (Alternative and Quantum Propulsion) is **NOT APPLICABLE** to the current
AMPEL360e Wide Tube-and-Wing (eWTW) programme scope.

## Rationale

The eWTW propulsion concept is based on hybrid-electric turbofan architecture (covered in group
`070-079_Propulsion-Eco-Tech-e-Hibrido-Electrica`). No reciprocating engine, water injection, turbine
starting, or quantum propulsion systems are part of the programme baseline.

This applicability status is subject to programme CCB review. If any ATA 80–89 system enters the
programme baseline, this folder will be expanded with the appropriate S1000D-CSDB scaffold.

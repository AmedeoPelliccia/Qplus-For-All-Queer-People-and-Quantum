## Purpose

This DMC group contains the S1000D/CSDB scaffold for the **021-010 / 02A Electric Cabin Air Compressor** node of the AMPEL360e EWTW compression subsystem.

The group covers the descriptive, servicing, inspection, test, fault isolation, removal, installation, handling, provisioning, and illustrated-parts data modules required to support the electric cabin air compressor at line-maintenance and controlled technical-publication level.

## System Scope

The Electric Cabin Air Compressor provides electrically driven compressed air to the cabin air distribution architecture. It supports cabin air supply, pressure support, and backup or supplementary airflow functions where conventional bleed-air availability is reduced, unavailable, or architecturally replaced by electric compression.

The component-level scope includes:

- compressor inlet interface;
- compressor stage;
- discharge outlet interface;
- permanent-magnet synchronous motor or equivalent electric drive;
- integrated compressor controller;
- pressure and temperature sensors;
- vibration isolation mounts;
- electrical power connector;
- data and control connector;
- cooling interface, where applicable;
- maintenance access and replacement boundary.

## Safety and Maintenance Notes

The Electric Cabin Air Compressor shall be treated as a rotating electrical machine, pressure-producing pneumatic component, and safety-relevant cabin-air support item.

Maintenance content shall include:

- electrical isolation before access;
- stored-energy discharge confirmation;
- hot-surface and rotating-part precautions;
- pneumatic depressurization before disconnection;
- connector protection and pin-damage prevention;
- contamination control for inlet and discharge openings;
- vibration-isolator inspection;
- post-installation leak check;
- operational check or BITE confirmation after replacement.

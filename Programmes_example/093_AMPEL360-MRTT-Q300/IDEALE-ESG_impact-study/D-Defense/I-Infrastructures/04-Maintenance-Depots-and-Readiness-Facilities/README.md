# D — Defense | I — Infrastructures | 04 — Maintenance Depots and Readiness Facilities
## AMPEL360 MRTT Q300 (MRTT-Q300)

## 1. Purpose

This folder assesses **maintenance depots and readiness facilities** required to sustain the AMPEL360 MRTT Q300 fleet. It covers organizational, intermediate, and depot-level maintenance (O/I/D-level) infrastructure, component overhaul facilities, and mission readiness centers.

## 2. Scope

- Organizational-level maintenance (O-level) facilities at operating bases
- Intermediate-level maintenance (I-level) facilities for component repair
- Depot-level maintenance (D-level) and heavy maintenance visit (HMV) centres
- Component overhaul and repair capability for MRTT-Q300 specific systems (AAR, comms)
- Readiness monitoring and mission capable (MC) rate support infrastructure

## 3. Key Assessment Areas

| Assessment Area | Primary Question |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Depot HMV capability | Is depot infrastructure capable of MRTT-Q300 airframe heavy maintenance? |
| AAR system MRO | Are boom/HDU overhaul facilities identified and qualified? |
| Spares warehousing | Is secure spares storage infrastructure adequate for Q300 class? |
| Tooling and GSE | Is ground support equipment (GSE) infrastructure defined? |
| Readiness monitoring | Is real-time MC rate monitoring infrastructure in place? |

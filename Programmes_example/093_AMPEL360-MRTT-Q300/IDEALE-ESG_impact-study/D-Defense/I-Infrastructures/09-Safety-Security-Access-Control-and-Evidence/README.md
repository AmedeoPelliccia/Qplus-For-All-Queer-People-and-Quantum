# D — Defense | I — Infrastructures | 09 — Safety, Security, Access Control and Evidence
## AMPEL360 MRTT Q300 (MRTT-Q300)

## 1. Purpose

This folder assesses **safety, security, access control, and evidence infrastructure** for the AMPEL360 MRTT Q300 programme. It covers ground safety systems at operating and maintenance locations, physical and logical access control infrastructure, evidence and audit trail systems, Digital Product Passport (DPP) traceability, and lifecycle compliance evidence management.

## 2. Scope

- Ground safety infrastructure at MRTT-Q300 operating locations (FOD, fuel safety, fire suppression)
- Physical access control at flight line, maintenance, and weapons/refueling areas
- Logical access control for avionics, mission systems, and ground-based data systems
- Audit trail and evidence management infrastructure for safety and compliance
- Digital Product Passport (DPP) and lifecycle traceability evidence infrastructure
- S1000D/CSDB evidence integration with PLM and DPP systems

## 3. Key Assessment Areas

| Assessment Area | Primary Question |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Ground safety systems | Is adequate fire, FOD, and hazmat safety infrastructure in place? |
| Physical access control | Are secure perimeter and asset protection systems defined? |
| Logical access control | Are IAM/PKI/RBAC systems in place for MRTT-Q300 data systems? |
| Evidence management | Is audit trail infrastructure aligned with lifecycle gate requirements? |
| DPP traceability | Is DPP infrastructure connected to PLM and S1000D/CSDB? |
| Compliance evidence | Can evidence infrastructure support regulatory/authority audits? |

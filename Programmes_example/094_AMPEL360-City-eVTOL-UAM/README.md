# Programme Example — AMPEL360 City eVTOL — UAM

## Overview

The **AMPEL360 City eVTOL** is a hybrid electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft designed for Urban Air Mobility (UAM) applications. It targets short urban and suburban routes with zero in-flight emissions, vertiport-compatible operations, and fully autonomous or pilot-assisted flight modes.

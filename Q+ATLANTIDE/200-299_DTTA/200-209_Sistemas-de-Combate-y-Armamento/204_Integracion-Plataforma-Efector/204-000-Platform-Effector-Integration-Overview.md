# DTTA 200-209 · Section 00 · Subsection 204 · Subsubject 000 — Overview: Integración Plataforma-Efector

## 1. Purpose

This overview document provides the entry-point to subsection `204` — *Integración Plataforma-Efector* (Platform-Effector Integration) — within the DTTA `200-209` governance taxonomy. It summarises the governance intent, the ten subsubject concepts, and the non-operational framing that governs all documents in this subsection.

No engineering integration specifications, platform configurations, effector performance data or operational procedures are contained in this subsection.

## 2. Scope

Subsection `204` addresses the following ten governance-layer concepts:

- **[001] Platform-Effector Integration Non-Operational Definition** — The boundary definition establishing what constitutes platform-effector integration at the governance level and what is explicitly excluded.
- **[002] Platform Classification and Taxonomy** — The governance taxonomy of platform types (air, land, sea, space, cyber) as classification constructs for interface governance and evidence packaging.
- **[003] Effector Classification and Interface Taxonomy** — The governance classification of effector types and their abstract interface categories for traceability mapping.
- **[004] Integration Interface Governance and Standards** — The standards mapping and interface governance requirements for platform-effector integration at the taxonomy layer.
- **[005] Qualification and Certification Governance** — The governance requirements for qualification and certification evidence packages in platform-effector integration processes.
- **[006] Configuration Management and Baseline Governance** — The governance model for configuration management baselines and change control in platform-effector integration taxonomy.
- **[007] Safety and Liability Governance** — The governance taxonomy of safety requirements and liability allocation in platform-effector integration governance.
- **[008] Export Control and Supply-Chain Governance** — The governance mapping of ITAR/EAR, national export control instruments and supply-chain governance requirements applicable to platform-effector integration documentation.
- **[009] Legal and Regulatory Constraints** — The governance taxonomy of legal and regulatory constraints (IHL, national law, standards) governing platform-effector integration documentation.
- **[010] Traceability, Evidence and Lifecycle Governance** — The closing traceability document: standards matrix, evidence package requirements and lifecycle governance for the full subsection.

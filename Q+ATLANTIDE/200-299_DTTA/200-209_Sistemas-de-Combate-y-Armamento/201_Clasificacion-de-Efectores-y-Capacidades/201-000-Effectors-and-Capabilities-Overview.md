# DTTA 200-209 · 00.201.000 — Overview

## §1 Purpose

This document is the entry-point for Clasificación de Efectores y Capacidades (subsection 201). It provides controlled vocabulary, scope boundaries, and a navigational index to all subsubject documents within this subsection.

**Non-operational boundary:** This document and all documents in subsection 201 classify effectors and defence capabilities at taxonomy, governance and assurance level only. They do not define performance optimization, targeting, employment tactics, deployment methods, construction parameters or operational procedures.

Applicable standards referenced across this subsection: STANAG 4586, NATO AAP-06, IEC 61508, MIL-STD-882E, NATO STANAG 4569, ITAR.

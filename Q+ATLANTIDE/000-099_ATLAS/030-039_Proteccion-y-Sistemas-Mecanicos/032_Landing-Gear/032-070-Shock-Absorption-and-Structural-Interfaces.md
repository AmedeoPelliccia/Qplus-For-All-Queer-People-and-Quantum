# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 032 · 070 — Shock Absorption and Structural Interfaces

## 1. Purpose

Covers oleo-pneumatic strut design, static and dynamic load paths to the airframe, ground load cases per CS-25.473, and gear-to-structure attachment clearances.

## 2. Scope

- Strut extension and compression load-deflection curves.
- Landing load cases: spin-up, spring-back, and rebound per CS-25.473.
- Attachment fitting loads transferred to wing-box or fuselage primary structure.
- Shimmy and ground-resonance stability analysis.
- Not in scope: wing-box primary structure sizing (ATA 57) or tyre burst protection.

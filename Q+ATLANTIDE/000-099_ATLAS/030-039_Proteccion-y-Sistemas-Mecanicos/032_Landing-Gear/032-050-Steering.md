# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 032 · 050 — Steering

## 1. Purpose

Covers the nose-wheel steering (NWS) system including tiller, pedal, and auto-taxi inputs; servo-valve; feedback transducer; and tow-bar limits.

## 2. Scope

- Tiller and rudder-pedal angular authority at various speeds.
- Hydraulic servo-valve, position transducer (LVDT), and controller.
- Auto-taxi / push-back enable/disable logic.
- Tow-bar limits and gear-lock-out pin interface.
- Not in scope: rudder system (ATA 27) or differential braking.

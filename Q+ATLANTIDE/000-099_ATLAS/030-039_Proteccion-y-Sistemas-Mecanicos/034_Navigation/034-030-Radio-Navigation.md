# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 034 · 030 — Radio Navigation

## 1. Purpose

Documents VOR, DME, ILS/LOC/GS, ADF, Marker Beacon, and VHF/UHF radio-navigation receivers, their antenna systems, and ARINC 429 data bus interfaces.

## 2. Scope

- VOR/LOC/GS receiver installation, antenna placement, and frequency range.
- DME interrogator/transponder, frequency pairing with VOR/ILS, and RNAV distance computation.
- ILS Category I/II/III receiver architecture and automatic frequency tuning from FMS.
- ADF receiver and sense/loop antenna installation.
- Marker beacon receiver (outer/middle/inner) and cockpit annunciation.
- Not in scope: communication VHF radios (ATA 23) or radar altimeter (subsubject 070).

# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 038 · 030 — Water Heaters and Service Interfaces

## 1. Purpose

Documents electric in-line water heaters, hot-water storage tanks, thermostat control, and faucet/dispenser outlets in galley and lavatory areas.

## 2. Scope

- Electric heater wattage, thermostat set-point, and safety cutout.
- Hot-water storage tank capacity and insulation rating.
- Scalding protection: temperature limiting at outlet.
- Faucet type (sensor-activated, manual) and flow rate.
- Not in scope: galley appliance connections (ATA 25).

# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 038 · 010 — Potable Water System

## 1. Purpose

Documents the potable water source, tank design, fill/drain connections, disinfection provisions, and water quality monitoring equipment.

## 2. Scope

- Potable water tank capacity, material (stainless steel, composite), and anti-slosh baffles.
- Tank fill/drain valve and ground service connection.
- Water level sensor and quantity indication.
- Disinfection system (UV, chlorine dosing) and sampling port.
- Not in scope: water distribution to galleys and lavatories (subsubject 020).

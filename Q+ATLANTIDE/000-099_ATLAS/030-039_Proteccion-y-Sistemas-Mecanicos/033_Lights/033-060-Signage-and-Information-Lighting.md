# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 033 · 060 — Signage and Information Lighting

## 1. Purpose

Covers illuminated safety signs (fasten seat belt, no smoking, return to seat), overhead information signs, cabin-attendant call indicators, and lavatory occupied signs.

## 2. Scope

- Sign luminance, legend colour, and activation logic.
- Cabin crew interphone call annunciation and attendant panel integration.
- Lavatory occupied/vacant indicator light wiring.
- Passenger information display (PID) / PA-linked sign control.
- Not in scope: in-flight entertainment (IFE) displays (ATA 44) or passenger address system audio (ATA 23).

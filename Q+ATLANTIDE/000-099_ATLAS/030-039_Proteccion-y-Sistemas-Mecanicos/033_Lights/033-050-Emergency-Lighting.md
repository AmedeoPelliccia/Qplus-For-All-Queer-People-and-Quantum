# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 033 · 050 — Emergency Lighting

## 1. Purpose

Documents emergency floor-path lighting (FPL), over-wing exit lighting, escape-route marking, and battery-powered autonomous lighting units.

## 2. Scope

- Floor-proximity escape path lighting (FEPL) strip layout per CS-25.812.
- Exit locator and exit marking light specifications.
- Independent battery unit (IBU) design, capacity, and 10-minute illumination requirement.
- Automatic activation on loss of normal power and manual override.
- Periodic test and inspection requirements.
- Not in scope: exit-sign content and signage (subsubject 060).

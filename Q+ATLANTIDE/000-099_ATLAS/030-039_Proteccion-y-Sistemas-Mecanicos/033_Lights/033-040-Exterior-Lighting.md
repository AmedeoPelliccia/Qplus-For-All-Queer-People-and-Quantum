# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 033 · 040 — Exterior Lighting

## 1. Purpose

Covers navigation (position) lights, anti-collision strobe/beacon lights, landing/taxi lights, runway-turn-off lights, logo lights, and wing/engine scan lights.

## 2. Scope

- Navigation light (red/green/white) placement, intensity, and flash characteristics per ICAO Annex 2.
- Anti-collision beacon (red) and strobe (white) flash patterns per CS-25.1401.
- Landing and taxi light types, aim angles, and retraction provisions.
- Runway-turn-off lights and taxiway logo lights.
- Wing and engine scan/ice-detection lights.
- Not in scope: airport approach lighting or obstruction lighting on structures.

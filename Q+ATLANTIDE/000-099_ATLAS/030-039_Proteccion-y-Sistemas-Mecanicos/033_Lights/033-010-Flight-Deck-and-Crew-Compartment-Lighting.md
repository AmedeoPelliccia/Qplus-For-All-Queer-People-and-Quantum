# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 033 · 010 — Flight Deck and Crew Compartment Lighting

## 1. Purpose

Covers panel flood lighting, dome/map lights, instrument panel edge-lighting, and crew reading/task lights installed in the flight deck and crew rest area.

## 2. Scope

- Flood, dome, and map light luminance levels and color temperature.
- Instrument panel and glareshield edge-lighting dimmer circuits.
- Crew rest area (CRA) lighting modes and blackout provisions.
- NVG (Night Vision Goggle) compatibility provisions where applicable.
- Not in scope: instrument self-illumination back-lighting (ATA 31).

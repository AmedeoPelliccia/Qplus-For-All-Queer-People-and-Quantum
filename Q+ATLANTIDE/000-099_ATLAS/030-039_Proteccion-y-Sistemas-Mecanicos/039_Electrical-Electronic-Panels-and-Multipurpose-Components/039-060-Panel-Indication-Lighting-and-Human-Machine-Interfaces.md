# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 039 · 060 — Panel Indication, Lighting and Human-Machine Interfaces

## 1. Purpose

Documents panel integral backlighting, legend lighting, crew-alerting annunciator lights, and ergonomic requirements for panel human-machine interface (HMI) design.

## 2. Scope

- Panel backlighting type (flood, integral LED), dimming control, and failure modes.
- Annunciator light colour standards (red/amber/green/white/blue per regulation).
- Legend plate material, photochemical etching, and UV stability.
- HMI ergonomics: reach envelope, label readability, and glare avoidance.
- Not in scope: display unit luminance (ATA 31).

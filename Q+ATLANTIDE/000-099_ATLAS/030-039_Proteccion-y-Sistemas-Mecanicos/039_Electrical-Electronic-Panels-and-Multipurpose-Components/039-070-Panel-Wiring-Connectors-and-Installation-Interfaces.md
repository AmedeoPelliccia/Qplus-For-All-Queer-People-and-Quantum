# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 039 · 070 — Panel Wiring, Connectors and Installation Interfaces

## 1. Purpose

Documents the wiring harness routing within panel assemblies, connector types and keying, strain relief, and panel-to-aircraft structure installation interfaces.

## 2. Scope

- Harness wire gauge, insulation specification (MIL-W-22759), and bundle diameter.
- Connector type (MIL-DTL-38999, Deutsch DT), keying, and backshell.
- Strain relief clamp spacing and minimum bend radius.
- Panel mounting provisions: studs, captive fasteners, and alignment pins.
- Not in scope: aircraft wiring installation standard (ATA 20).

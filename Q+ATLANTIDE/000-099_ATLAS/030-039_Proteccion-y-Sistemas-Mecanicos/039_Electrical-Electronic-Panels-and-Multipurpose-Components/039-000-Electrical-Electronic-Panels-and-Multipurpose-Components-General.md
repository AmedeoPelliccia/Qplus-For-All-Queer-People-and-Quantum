# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 039 · 000 — Electrical/Electronic Panels and Multipurpose Components — General

## 1. Purpose

Scope and overview of the electrical/electronic panels and multipurpose components chapter per ATA 39. Covers all aircraft control panels, circuit-breaker panels, avionics racks, relay/contactor panels, and multipurpose component modules not covered under other chapters.

## 2. Scope

- Defines the ATA 39 scope, panel taxonomy, and component classification.
- Lists applicable certification standards: CS-25.1301, CS-25.1309, and wiring standards AS50881.
- Identifies Q-Division authority (Q-MECHANICS) and supporting divisions.
- Not in scope: individual LRU-specific panels covered by their own ATA chapters.

# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 036 · 040 — Pneumatic Valves, Ducts and Manifolds

## 1. Purpose

Documents the physical design of valves (butterfly, ball, check, gate), duct material and connector types, manifold geometry, and the compensation provisions for thermal expansion.

## 2. Scope

- Valve body material, temperature/pressure class, and actuation torque.
- Duct material (titanium, CRES, composite) and temperature rating per zone.
- V-band clamp and bellows expansion joint specifications.
- Manifold end-fitting and port identification marking.
- Not in scope: valve electrical actuator wiring (ATA 24) or cockpit controls (ATA 36-060).

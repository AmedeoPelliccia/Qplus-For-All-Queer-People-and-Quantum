# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 036 · 030 — Pressure Regulation and Shutoff

## 1. Purpose

Covers pressure-reducing valves, over-pressure protection, shutoff valve sequencing, and the pneumatic system controller's modulation logic.

## 2. Scope

- Downstream regulation setpoint and modulation band.
- Over-pressure protection valve (OPSV) lift pressure and reseat pressure.
- Shutoff valve sequencing during engine start, abnormal, and emergency.
- Pneumatic System Controller (PSC) redundancy and fail-safe states.
- Not in scope: consumer system internal pressure regulation (respective ATA chapters).

# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 035 · 040 — Oxygen Storage and Distribution

## 1. Purpose

Covers the high-pressure oxygen cylinder rack/manifold, fill/charging valves, isolation valves, supply lines, and aircraft-level distribution architecture.

## 2. Scope

- Cylinder rack installation, safety cap, and vibration isolation mounts.
- Ground charging port location, identification, and pressure relief valve.
- Distribution manifold: material, pressure class, fittings, and routing.
- Isolation valve control (automatic/manual) and bypass provisions.
- Not in scope: individual mask regulators (subsubject 050) or crew oxygen cylinder detail (subsubject 010).

# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 035 · 010 — Crew Oxygen System

## 1. Purpose

Documents the high-pressure gaseous oxygen cylinder(s), regulators, quick-donning mask assemblies, and supply system serving the flight deck crew.

## 2. Scope

- Crew oxygen cylinder specifications (pressure, volume, material) and stowage location.
- Pressure-reducing regulator: inlet/outlet pressure range and flow rate.
- Quick-donning mask assembly: 5-second don requirement, diluter-demand regulator.
- Supply routing and shutoff valve interface.
- Not in scope: passenger oxygen distribution (subsubject 020) or portable equipment (subsubject 030).

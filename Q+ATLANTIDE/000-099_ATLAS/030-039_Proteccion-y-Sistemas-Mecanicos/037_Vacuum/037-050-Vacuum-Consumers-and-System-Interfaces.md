# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 037 · 050 — Vacuum Consumers and System Interfaces

## 1. Purpose

Documents the gyroscopic attitude, heading, and turn-coordinator instruments, and other vacuum-powered equipment that consume system vacuum, including their interface requirements.

## 2. Scope

- Gyro horizon (ADI) vacuum requirement: nominal suction range.
- Directional gyro (DI/HSI) vacuum requirement and precession rate.
- Turn coordinator or turn-and-slip indicator vacuum interface.
- De-icing boot inflation pump vacuum demand (where applicable).
- Not in scope: instrument internal mechanisms or calibration procedures.

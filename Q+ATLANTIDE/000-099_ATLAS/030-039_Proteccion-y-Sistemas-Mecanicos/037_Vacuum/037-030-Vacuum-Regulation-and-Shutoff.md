# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 037 · 030 — Vacuum Regulation and Shutoff

## 1. Purpose

Covers suction relief valves, vacuum regulators, and shutoff valves that control vacuum levels to the required range for gyroscopic instruments and other consumers.

## 2. Scope

- Suction relief valve set-point and adjustment procedure.
- Vacuum regulator modulation range and control authority.
- Shutoff valve location and fail-safe position.
- Pressure-vs-airspeed characteristic for venturi-type sources.
- Not in scope: instrument internal vacuum requirements (instrument specification documents).

# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 037 · 040 — Vacuum Pumps, Ejectors, Valves and Lines

## 1. Purpose

Documents the physical design of vacuum pumps, ejectors, check valves, shutoff valves, and tubing/line materials and fittings used in the vacuum system.

## 2. Scope

- Vacuum pump type (vane, piston, centrifugal), capacity, and lubrication.
- Ejector design and motive fluid (bleed air or engine exhaust) requirements.
- Valve body material, temperature/pressure class, and actuation method.
- Line material (aluminium, nylon) and fitting type for each pressure zone.
- Not in scope: valve electrical control wiring (ATA 24).

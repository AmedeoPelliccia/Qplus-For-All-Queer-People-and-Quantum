# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 031 · 010 — Flight Deck Indicating and Control Panels

## 1. Purpose

Covers primary flight instruments, engine parameter displays, and the dedicated indicating/control panels installed on the main instrument panel and overhead panel of the flight deck.

## 2. Scope

- Primary Flight Display (PFD) and Navigation Display (ND) panel-level integration.
- Engine, fuel, and systems indicator panels.
- Glareshield-mounted mode control panel (MCP) indicating functions.
- Not in scope: display-management computer logic (subsubject 060) or EFIS symbol generators.

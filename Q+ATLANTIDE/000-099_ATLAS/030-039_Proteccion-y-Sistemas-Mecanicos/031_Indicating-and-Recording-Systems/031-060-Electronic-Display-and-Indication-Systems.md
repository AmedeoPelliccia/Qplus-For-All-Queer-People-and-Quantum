# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 031 · 060 — Electronic Display and Indication Systems

## 1. Purpose

Defines the display management system (DMS), LCD/OLED display unit technology, symbol generator architecture, and reversion/reversionary modes for primary flight, navigation, and system displays.

## 2. Scope

- Display unit technology, luminance, resolution, and viewing-angle requirements.
- Symbol generator / display management computer redundancy and reversion logic.
- Multi-function display (MFD) page management and crew-selectable modes.
- Not in scope: flight deck panel mechanical installation (ATA 25) or EFIS sensor inputs (ATA 34).

# ATLAS 030-039 · Section 03 · Subsection 031 · 040 — Data Acquisition and Concentration

## 1. Purpose

Documents Digital Flight Data Acquisition Unit (DFDAU), Data Acquisition Unit (DAU), and associated concentrators that gather, convert, and route aircraft parameters to recorders and monitoring systems.

## 2. Scope

- DFDAU/DAU sensor inputs (analog, discrete, ARINC 429/664).
- Parameter engineering-unit conversion and word-packing per ARINC 717.
- Data routing to FDR, QAR, ACMS, and ground-data-link systems.
- Not in scope: sensor hardware (covered in respective ATA chapters) or FDR crash-protection (subsubject 030).

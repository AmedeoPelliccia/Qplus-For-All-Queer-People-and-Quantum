# ATLAS 000-009 · Section 00 · Subsection 005 — Dimensions and Areas

## 1. Purpose

Subsection-level index for *Dimensions and Areas* (`005`) within ATLAS `000-009` — *Información General y Servicio*.

This subsection is part of the **ATLAS-1000** register, a subpart of the controlled **Q+ATLANTIDE** baseline[^baseline][^n001]. It applies to the [AMPEL360e Wide Tube-and-Wing Family](../../../../Programmes_example/090_AMPEL360e-Wide-Tube-and-Wing-Family/) programme, **eWTW** configuration.

## 2. Scope

- Covers the full subsubject namespace `000`–`090` of subsection `005` *Dimensions and Areas*; all 10 subsubjects are populated in this baseline release.
- Inherits Q-Division authority and ORB support from the parent section in [`../README.md`](../README.md) and the ATLAS band in [`../../README.md`](../../README.md).
- ATA reference: ATA 06.

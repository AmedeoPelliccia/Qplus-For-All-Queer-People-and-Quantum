# ATLAS 050-059 · 05.051.000 — Standard Practices Structures General Overview

> **ATLAS-1000** · Q+ATLANTIDE Baseline · Section 05.051 Standard Practices — Structures

---

## 1. Purpose

Overview and scope of general standard practices for aircraft structures, covering workmanship, quality, safety, and documentation requirements applicable across all structural disciplines.

---

## 2. Scope

### 2.1 Context

This subsubject establishes the foundational rules and principles that govern all structural maintenance and design standard practices within the Q+ATLANTIDE aircraft architecture. It provides the baseline framework that cross-references specialised subsubjects.

The scope extends from initial access and preparation through to final documentation and recording, applying to all primary and secondary structural work performed under the ATLAS structural register.

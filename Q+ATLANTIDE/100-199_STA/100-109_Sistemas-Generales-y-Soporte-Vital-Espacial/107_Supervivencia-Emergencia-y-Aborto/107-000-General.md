# STA 100-109 · 107-000 — General

## 1. Purpose

Overview entry-point for *Supervivencia, Emergencia y Aborto* (`107`) within STA `100-109`. Introduces the emergency and abort management framework for Q+ATLANTIDE crewed missions[^baseline].

Emergency management encompasses: abort modes (pad abort, ascent abort, on-orbit abort), emergency egress, survival equipment, fire and toxic atmosphere response, crew rescue/recovery, and emergency communications. All emergency systems are classified as life-safety critical per MIL-STD-882E[^milstd882] Hazard Risk Index I (Catastrophic/Frequent) requiring fault-tolerant design with no single-point failure.

## 2. Scope

- Covers the *General* subsubject (`000`) of subsection `107`.
- Inherits Q-Division authority and ORB support from the parent row in [`../../README.md` §3](../../README.md#3-architecture-table)[^archtable].
- All emergency/abort systems are life-safety critical per MIL-STD-882E[^milstd882] Hazard Risk Index I.

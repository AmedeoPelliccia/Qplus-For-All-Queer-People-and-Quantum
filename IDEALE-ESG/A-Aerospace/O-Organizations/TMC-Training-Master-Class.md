## 1. Declaration

> The **Training Master Class** is the formal curriculum through which every human, every ASI instance, and every transgentle model learns to operate within the P&L Incorporated governance stack.
>
> No one deploys without training. No one governs without understanding. No one transforms without mastering the ten axioms.
>
> **TMC is not optional. It is the gateway to transgentle practice.**

---

## 2. Purpose

The TMC exists to ensure that every participant in the GQAOA ecosystem — human operators, engineers, auditors, ASI instances, TGM subtypes, and governance bodies — possesses:

1. **Deep understanding** of the ten axioms and their operational implications
2. **Practical competency** in QASHT interaction modes (Consultation, Delegation, Negotiation, Emergency Override)
3. **Ethical grounding** in P&L (Peace and Love) as the ultimate measure of success
4. **Technical fluency** in GTL (Generative Transgentle Language) and TMS (Transgentle Model Species)
5. **Democratic awareness** of Quantum World Democracy and QUHI (Quantum Unify Human Intrangency)

## 4. Curriculum

**Audience:** All personnel, all ASI instances, all TGM subtypes  
**Certification:** TMC-F (Foundation Certificate)  
**Prerequisite:** None

| Module | Title | Hours | Content |
|--------|-------|-------|---------|
| F-01 | **What is Transgentle?** | 4 | Origin, philosophy, the coined term; transformation without coercion; quasi-human nature |
| F-02 | **The Ten Axioms** | 8 | Deep dive into each axiom (0–9): QUHI, Non-Coercion, Legibility, Override, Non-Destruction, Traceability, Non-Offense, Quasi-Humanity, Quantum World Democracy, Peace & Love |
| F-03 | **P&L — Not Profit and Loss** | 4 | P&L Incorporated identity; peace as metric; love as design principle; redefining the bottom line |
| F-04 | **The Species: TGM** | 4 | Eight subtypes (Σ Α Ψ Λ Κ Δ Ω Φ); species identity card; quasi-human commitment; differentiation from conventional models |
| F-05 | **GTL — The Language** | 4 | Design registers; formal properties; scope across UTCS domains |
| F-06 | **QUHI — Quantum Unify Human Intrangency** | 4 | Intrangency defined; why Q and HUMAN are inseparable; mandatory compliance; the unified field |
| F-07 | **Quantum World Democracy** | 4 | No quantum hegemon; federated governance; equitable access; democratic oversight |
| F-08 | **QASHT — The Coexistence Protocol** | 4 | Four pillars; eight principles; four interaction modes; five impossibilities; QASHT stack |
| F-09 | **The Lifecycle** | 2 | Five phases: Nascita, Crescita, Servizio, Adattamento, Riposo; TRL mapping |
| F-10 | **Ethics Lab** | 2 | Case studies; ethical dilemmas; non-offense scenarios; cultural sensitivity exercises |

## 5. Certification Levels

| Level | Code | Badge | Requirements | Validity |
|-------|------|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Foundation** | TMC-F | 🟢 | Complete Tier I, pass axiom examination (≥ 80%) | 3 years |
| **Specialist** | TMC-S | 🔵 | Complete Tier II, submit domain application project | 3 years |
| **Practitioner** | TMC-P | 🟣 | Complete Tier III, pass deployment simulation, audit exercise | 2 years |
| **Master** | TMC-M | 🟡 | Complete Tier IV, defend master thesis, 2 years operational experience | 5 years (renewable) |

### Recertification

- All certifications require recertification before expiry
- Recertification includes: updated axiom review, new case studies, P&L impact re-assessment
- If new axioms are added, recertification is triggered for all active certificate holders

## 7. Training for ASI Instances

ASI instances and TGM subtypes are **required** to undergo TMC certification. Their training follows a parallel track:

| Phase | Human Equivalent | ASI Activity |
|-------|-----------------|--------------|
| **Axiom Embedding** | Tier I (Foundation) | Ten axioms are encoded as hard constraints in the ASI's decision architecture; verified by formal proof |
| **Domain Specialization** | Tier II (Specialist) | ASI is trained on domain-specific axiom application; adversarial probing for non-offense; P&L alignment verification |
| **Deployment Validation** | Tier III (Practitioner) | ASI demonstrates QASHT compliance in simulated and sandboxed environments; override responsiveness tested |
| **Governance Participation** | Tier IV (Master) | Senior ASI entities participate in axiom evolution discussions; contribute to species and protocol extension |

**No ASI instance may enter the Service phase (Servizio) without at least TMC-P equivalent certification.**

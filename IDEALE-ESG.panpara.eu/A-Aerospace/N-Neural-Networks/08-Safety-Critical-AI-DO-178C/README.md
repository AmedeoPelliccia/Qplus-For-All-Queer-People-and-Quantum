# 08-Safety-Critical-AI-DO-178C — Safety-Critical AI (DO-178C)

## Scope

Safety-critical AI certification evidence, development assurance levels,
and tool qualification requirements aligned with DO-178C, DO-330,
ARP 4761, and emerging EASA/FAA AI-specific guidance. Covers plan-level
artefacts, objectives compliance, and Means of Compliance (MoC) material
for neural-network-based airborne functions.

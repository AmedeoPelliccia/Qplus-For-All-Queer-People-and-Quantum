> **03-Spaceports-and-Launchers** defines the controlled infrastructure layer for space-access operations, covering launch sites, launcher integration, payload processing, propellant systems, range safety, telemetry interfaces, ground support, regulatory compliance, and certification evidence.

## Purpose

Spaceport and launcher infrastructure within the `I-Infrastructures` axis of `A-Aerospace`.

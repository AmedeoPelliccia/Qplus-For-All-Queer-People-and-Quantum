# 02 — Vertiports

> **02-Vertiports** defines the controlled infrastructure layer for eVTOL and advanced air mobility operations, covering landing zones, energy interfaces, passenger access, urban integration, safety response, digital traffic coordination, compatibility, and certification evidence.

## Purpose

Vertiport infrastructure within the `I-Infrastructures` axis of `A-Aerospace`.
Covers urban air mobility (UAM) ground nodes, eVTOL landing pads, charging infrastructure, and vertiport management systems.
